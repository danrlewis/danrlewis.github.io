About Daniel Probably not a robot.

I am a native Floridian, a fact I don't share readily. I initially pursued teaching, but fell in love with design after finding it far more rewarding than being regularly berated by sweaty 6th graders and bribing kindergarteners with pizza slices to do their homework. I spent most of 2008 in Denver, Colorado, my only souvenir from that time being a Barack Obama action figure I purchased on the street during the DNC. I am sarcastic yet also honest to a fault. I lived in Portland, Oregon for six years, a beautiful but unsufferable utopia of white, psuedo-libertarian homogeneity. I have a dog named Benny. If I could write a love letter to NYC, I imagine it would somehow manage to be florid yet perfunctory.
