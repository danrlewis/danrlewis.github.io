why daniel

100% human

Daniel has sustainably sourced, state of the art organs suspended in patented fibrous membranes, a fully functioning brain and four flexible human limbs. So talented.

Definitely not a robot

Robots are evil, murderous machines who kidnap and sacrifice human children to please their robot overlord. Daniel will never sacrifice your children to any robot overlords.

Codes proficiently

Not only has Daniel designed things, he is also a proficient front-end developer who has worked on countless Rails apps. Whatever those are.

Lives in NYC

Daniel is not simply human, he's an incredibly smart human. Because if he's going to apply for jobs in NYC, it can be very important to live there.

Experienced

With over seven years of both product and agency design experience, Daniel is well suited for senior roles. Like director of sugary snacks.
